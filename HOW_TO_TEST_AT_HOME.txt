# How to Test at Home

1. **Unzip** the `SBC_WebMap_v17.zip` file to a folder (e.g., on your Desktop).
2. **Open** that folder.

## Testing on PC
*   Double-click `run_map.bat`.
*   It will open the map in your default browser.

## Testing on Mobile (WiFi)
1.  Make sure your PC and Phone are on the **same WiFi**.
2.  Double-click `run_map_https.bat`.
    *   It may ask to install a library (`cryptography`) - let it do so.
    *   **Firewall:** If Windows Firewall pops up, click **"Allow Access"** (Private Networks).
3.  The text window will show an address like:
    `Network: https://192.168.1.15:8443`
4.  **On your Phone:**
    *   Open Chrome or Safari.
    *   Type that exact address (including the `https://` and `:8443`).
    *   **Warning:** You will see a "Not Secure" or "Connection is not private" warning.
        *   **Chrome:** Click "Advanced" -> "Proceed to...".
        *   **Safari:** Click "Show Details" -> "visit this website".
5.  The map should load! Click the "Locate Me" button to see your GPS position.
